## 1. The pedagogy — why Morechard is different

Most financial education, including excellent existing resources, teaches money through relatable but **hypothetical** scenarios: *imagine you're saving for a birthday gift.* Morechard inverts this. The app delivers the right lesson at the **moment a child actually earns, saves or spends their own money**, so the learning attaches to a real, personally-meaningful experience rather than a worksheet.

This is grounded in **experiential / situated learning**: knowledge anchored to a learner's own authentic experience is retained and transferred far more effectively than decontextualised instruction. The activity-tracking within the app exists for one reason — to generate those genuine teachable moments and trigger the relevant lesson in real time.

## 3. Privacy and safeguarding — built in, not bolted on

Morechard collects **no children's personal data** — no names, emails, addresses or dates of birth. Child accounts are paired via anonymous, parent-generated tokens. The product is GDPR and GDPR-K compliant by design, removing the data-handling concerns that typically slow school adoption.
