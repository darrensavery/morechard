- **Product in 1 sentence:** A fintech "Truth Engine" PWA for separated families that provides legal-grade financial records and AI-powered behavioral coaching.
- **Target user(s):** Separated/co-parenting families, unrepresented litigants in family court (80%), children ages 10–16, and the UK-Polish expat corridor.
