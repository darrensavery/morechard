FROM NOTEBOOKLM — BUSINESS STRATEGY EXPORT

**1) Strategy Snapshot**
- **Product in 1 sentence:** A chore tracker for families — including separated and co-parenting households — with an integrated financial literacy layer powered by real usage data and a high-integrity ledger underneath.
- **Target user(s):** Nuclear and separated/co-parenting families; children ages 10–16 (primary launch cohort); unrepresented family court litigants; the UK-Polish expat corridor.
- **Core problem:** Families lack a simple, shared, trustworthy way to track chores and the money attached to them — friction that compounds significantly when parents live in separate households.
- **Differentiation:** Chore-first simplicity that works for any family structure, with a financial literacy curriculum driven by the child's own data, and an immutable SHA-256 ledger that doubles as a court-ready audit record for separated parents.
- **Key objective (next 3–6 months):** Win on chore-tracker usability for everyday families; use the integrity layer as the upsell story for co-parenting and separated household segments.

**2) Market & Positioning**
- **Category:** Family Chore Tracker & Financial Literacy (distinct from "Allowance App" and "Family Finance" categories).
- **Competitors referenced:** RoosterMoney (NatWest), GoHenry, Gimi, OurFamilyWizard, AppClose.
- **Unique angle:** Chore-first UX any family can adopt on day one; financial literacy content grounded in the child's real data; audit-grade records that serve separated households without making intact families feel like a secondary audience.
- **Why now:** Existing apps are either too banking-centric (debit card required) or too co-parenting-niche to appeal broadly. Morechard is the first that works simply for nuclear families and scales to separated households without a mode switch.

**3) Monetisation**
- **Pricing model:** "Solo-SaaS" model featuring a Lifetime License and annual AI subscription.
- **Subscription tiers:** £34.99 Lifetime License (Core Access) + £14.99 to £19.99/year (AI Service).
- **Free vs paid boundaries:** Reverse trial (14 or 31 days) providing full access, followed by a "Hard Lock" allowing only 'Buy' or 'Export Data' actions.
