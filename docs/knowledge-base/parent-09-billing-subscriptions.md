**How to do it:**

- **Morechard Shield (£149.99 one-time):** Everything in Core AI plus access to audit-ready cryptographic, hashed PDF exports to share with solicitors and mediators.
- **AI Mentor + Learning Lab upgrade (£29.99 one-time):** Adds AI Mentor and Learning Lab to an existing Morechard Core purchase.
- All prices shown in GBP. USD and PLN equivalents shown at checkout.
- All plans are one-time payments — no subscriptions, no renewals.

## How to upgrade your plan

**What this is:** How to move from Morechard Core to Core AI or Shield, or activate the AI Mentor + Learning Lab add-on.

**How to do it:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**.
2. Tap **Plans & Upgrades**.
3. Select the plan or upgrade you want and tap **Continue**.
4. Complete payment via Stripe.
5. Your plan upgrades immediately.

**Things to know:**
- All upgrades are one-time payments — no recurring charge.
- If you already hold Morechard Core, you can add AI Mentor + Learning Lab for £29.99 rather than purchasing Core AI at full price.
- Shield unlocks the full cryptographic PDF export suite for use with solicitors, mediators, and co-parent legal proceedings.

**Still need help?** [Contact our support team](https://morechard.freshdesk.com/support/tickets/new)

## How to access the Stripe billing portal

**What this is:** The Stripe portal is where you manage payment methods and view invoices.

**How to do it:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**.
2. Tap **Manage Billing** (opens the Stripe customer portal in your browser).
3. Update payment methods or download invoices from there.

**Things to know:**
- The Stripe portal is a separate Stripe-hosted page, not part of the Morechard app.
- Morechard does not store your card details — all payment data is held by Stripe.
- If you cannot access the portal, ensure you are using the same email address registered with Morechard.

**Still need help?** [Contact our support team](https://morechard.freshdesk.com/support/tickets/new)

## Activating the AI Mentor + Learning Lab add-on

**What this is:** Step-by-step activation for the AI Mentor + Learning Lab after purchasing as a standalone upgrade.

**How to do it:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**.
2. Tap **Plans & Upgrades**.
3. Select **Add AI Mentor + Learning Lab — £29.99** and complete payment via Stripe.
4. The Mentor and Learning Lab activate immediately. Your children will see them in the app.

**Things to know:**
- This upgrade is only available to families who already hold Morechard Core.
- One upgrade covers all children on your family account.
- Alternatively, purchase Morechard Core AI (£64.99) which includes Core + AI Mentor + Learning Lab in one purchase.

**Still need help?** [Contact our support team](https://morechard.freshdesk.com/support/tickets/new)
