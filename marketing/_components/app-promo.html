The app

A pocket money app that actually works - and teaches valuable financial concepts as it goes.

Morechard lives on your phone. Parents manage, children track - in one shared, secure space.

  • Assign & approve chores
    Set a chore, a rate, and a deadline. Your child marks it done - you approve and the ledger updates instantly - building responsibility and autonomy.
  • Real-time pocket money balance
    Children see their balance grow with every approved job. A live, honest account they own and can allocate to their chosen goals.
  • AI Mentor weekly briefing
    A real-time AI powered financial mentor, teaching your children the value of money, developed in consultation with a registered child psychologist and aligned with Money and Pensions Service financial education guidelines.
