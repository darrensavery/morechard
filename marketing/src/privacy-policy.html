Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Morechard ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

What we collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate the service: your email address (for account access and launch notifications), and anonymised usage data via PostHog to help us improve the product.

How we use your data

Your email is used solely for account management and, where you have consented, for product updates. We do not sell, share, or rent your data to third parties.

Data retention

You may delete your account at any time using the "Uproot" option in Settings. This permanently removes your personal data from our systems within 30 days, while retaining anonymised ledger records for hash-chain integrity.
