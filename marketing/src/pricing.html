Common questions

FAQ

Is this really a one-time payment? No subscription ever?

Yes — every plan is a single purchase. Once you pay, the software is yours. There are no renewal reminders, no price increases, and no cancellation flows. We deliberately built it this way because the families who need Morechard most — particularly separated households — shouldn’t have to worry about a payment lapsing and losing access to their records.

What happens when the 14-day trial ends?

Your data is safe. Your ledger remains intact and cryptographically sealed. You can still generate a summary data export at any time — we don’t hold records hostage. To continue using chore tracking, savings goals, and AI features, you’ll need to purchase any plan.

What does Core AI add over Core?

Core AI adds three things: Parent Insights — a weekly AI summary of your child’s consistency, earning velocity, and savings behaviour; the AI Mentor — personalised financial coaching for your child based on their real chore and earnings data; and the Learning Lab — a 20-module structured financial literacy curriculum mapped to your child’s age and progress.

When should I choose Shield AI over Core AI?

Shield AI is built for families who need a verifiable legal record — most commonly separated or co-parenting households. On top of everything in Core AI, it adds court-ready PDF exports with cryptographic tamper-seals, a co-parent verified sharing system, shared expense logging across two homes, and records designed to meet documentary evidence standards. UK family mediation averages £140/hr. Shield AI costs £149.99 once.

Can I upgrade later if I start on Core?

Yes. Core users can add the AI Mentor + Learning Lab bundle for a one-time £29.99 upgrade — no rebuy required. To move to Shield AI, you pay the difference at the time of upgrade. You never pay full price for something you’ve already bought.

Does Morechard work for single-parent households?

Absolutely — and most Core and Core AI users are single-household families. The co-parent features in Shield AI are entirely optional. If you don’t have a second household, those features simply won’t appear in your workflow. They don’t get in the way.

What currencies are supported at launch?

GBP (£), USD ($), and PLN (zł). Your currency is detected automatically from your region. Prices shown on this page are in GBP; USD and PLN equivalents are displayed at checkout.

Is there a refund policy?

The 14-day free trial exists precisely so you can evaluate Morechard with no financial commitment. Because you try before you buy, purchases are non-refundable. If something isn’t working as expected, contact us at hello@morechard.com and we’ll sort it out.
